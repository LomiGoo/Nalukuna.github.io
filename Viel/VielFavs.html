Foods Description
Sisig Sisig is a Filipino dish made from chopped, seasoned pork served hot with onions, chili, and calamansi.
Adobo Adobo is a Filipino meat dish cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and spices.
Sinigang Sinigang is a Filipino sour soup with pork, shrimp, or fish, flavored with tamarind and tomatoes.
